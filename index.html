why i love reading

Reading is one of my favourite activities because it allows me to escape into different worlds and learn new ideas. When I read, I feel calm and focused, and it helps me forget stress for a while. Books also teach me about people, cultures, and experiences I may never see in real life. I love how a good story can make me think deeply or feel strong emotions. Reading also improves my vocabulary and helps me express myself better. Whether it is fiction, self‑help, or pyschology, every book gives me something valuable. This is why reading is an important part of my life.
